The Javelin Missile Launcher, designed by Raytheon, was spec'd for engagement distances farther than was is being achieved.

I designed the tripod mount below for the Infantry Weapons and Anti-Armor Team of the Marines to increase the stability and therefore possible engagement distance of the launcher.

![[Pasted image 20250731132746.png]]![[Pasted image 20250731132748.png]]![[Pasted image 20250731132751.png]]

The rear support is necessary, but experiences minimal load. Being that it's essentially a cantilevered beam, I originally designed it to be a sheet of water jet cut quasi-isotropic carbon fiber plate.

FEA validation showed that with a minimal increase in thickness, 6061 was sufficient to handle any expected load cases
